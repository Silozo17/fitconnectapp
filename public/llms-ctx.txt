# FitConnect - Extended LLM Context

## Platform Overview

FitConnect (https://getfitconnect.co.uk) is the United Kingdom's premier fitness coach marketplace. The platform connects fitness-seeking individuals with certified professionals across multiple disciplines including personal training, boxing, MMA, nutrition, and bodybuilding.

## Target Audience

### Clients
- Individuals seeking personal training (online or in-person)
- People looking to lose weight, build muscle, or improve fitness
- Athletes seeking specialized coaching (boxing, MMA)
- Anyone needing nutrition guidance and meal planning
- Bodybuilding competitors seeking prep coaches

### Coaches
- Certified personal trainers seeking clients
- Boxing and MMA coaches
- Registered nutritionists and dietitians
- Bodybuilding coaches and prep specialists
- Fitness professionals wanting to sell digital products

## Core Features - Detailed

### Client Journey
1. **Discovery**: Browse coaches filtered by specialty, location, price range, ratings, and availability
2. **Profile Review**: View detailed coach profiles with qualifications, reviews, gallery, and services
3. **Booking**: Select session type (online/in-person), duration, and time slot
4. **Payment**: Secure checkout via Stripe with multiple payment methods
5. **Session**: Attend session via video call or in-person at specified location
6. **Progress**: Track fitness progress with integrated wearable data (Apple Health, Fitbit, Garmin)
7. **Review**: Leave feedback and ratings for completed sessions

### Coach Features
1. **Profile Management**: Create detailed professional profile with bio, qualifications, and portfolio
2. **Service Configuration**: Set up session types, durations, and pricing
3. **Availability**: Manage calendar and availability settings
4. **Client Management**: View client roster, progress data, and communication history
5. **Digital Products**: Create and sell workout plans, nutrition guides, and ebooks
6. **Invoicing**: Generate professional invoices with VAT support
7. **Analytics**: Track revenue, booking trends, and client retention

### Digital Marketplace
- Downloadable workout programs (PDF, video)
- Nutrition guides and meal plans
- Fitness ebooks and educational content
- Training plan templates
- Bundle packages combining multiple products
